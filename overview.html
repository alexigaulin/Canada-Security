About Us

We’ve been serving Southern Alberta since 1990. We’ve built our business on giving our clients great service, not keeping them through long term contracts. We are members of CANASA. We educate our customers so that they can make informed decisions. We are upfront and honest with our costs, and fees. We use quality products, and design systems to fit your needs and budget. We strive to eliminate false alarms. We are here to serve your security needs. We provide our clients with high quality, custom security solutions. From alarms to cameras, wireless or hardwired, we can customize a system to properly meet your specific needs and budget. We give prompt, courteous service and offer an extended service plan that can help prevent system trouble and reduce costly maintenance and down time.

Our Team

Our technicians are professional installers who are trained, certified, and bondable. We’re part of a select network of security experts with a proven track record of exceptional service. Our standards are higher and so is your security. Give us a call if you have any questions at all.

CANASA Member

The Canadian Security Association (CANASA) is the national non-profit organization, established in 1977, dedicated to promoting the interests of its members and the safety and security of all Canadians. They are the recognized voice of the security industry and have a growing membership of more than 1,300 companies across Canada, representing all segments of the industry. We have been a CANASA member for over 10 Years.
